    True Color is a online platform allowing customers design and customize logo directly on available promotional items such as shirt, cap, umbrella, bag, glass, and so on. After designing, customers can view their outcomes as real images by mockups.

    Our service mainly serve promotional websites (B2B), meaning we offer websites a design plugin to encourage customers design or upload logo by themselves. Even non-professional customers, they will be supported and motivated by simple and friendly functions without complex designing skills.

    Our Process

    For promotional website interested in our app,
    there are some takeaways you need to follow:

    • 1. Must add the app into your website
    • 2. Post empty samples which customers
      are able to customize on them
    • 3. A popup will appear when customer
      click “Create Visual Image”
    • 4. The orders will be saved and carted
      in the website’s storage
